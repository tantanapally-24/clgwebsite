Guest lectures

Alumni Meet is a gathering of passed out students of an institution and it is a place where the institution feels proud on seeing its successful alumni. During their meet, the alumni community shares their experience in the outside world, Which they faced after stepping out of the institution. The meet also creates a platform to identify the colleges most distinguished alumni.

A Welcome Message To Alumini

Dear Alumni,
It is my pleasure to welcome you to the alumni section of our website, where you can feel right at home. In this section, you can be active and engaged members of the community, reconnect with your classmates and keep up to date about alumni events.
I am tremendously proud of what you have all been accomplishing since leaving campus, and of how you - and the university - have grown in the past few years. As you continue on your paths, fulfilling your potential and acting as ambassadors for Vignan, I encourage you to think often on the mission Dr. Lavu Rathaiah set for Vignan's University and for you: to become leaders in the world, promoting peace and hope.
We look forward to your continued success.
Sincerely,
Chairman-Alumni Association

Webinars

A webinar is an online seminar that turns a presentation into a real-time conversation from anywhere in the world. Webinars allow large groups of participants to engage in online discussions or training events and share audio, documents or slides – even when they’re not in the same place as the meeting host or in the same room where the event or presentation is taking place.
